About Food Wastage Reduction Management System

The Food Wastage Reduction Management System is a web-based application designed to help organizations reduce food wastage by effectively managing food inventory, tracking waste events, implementing prevention measures, and generating insightful reports.

This system provides users with the tools they need to monitor their food inventory, identify areas of waste, implement strategies to reduce waste, and track the effectiveness of those strategies over time.
